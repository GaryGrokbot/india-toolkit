RTI TEMPLATE: AWBI Inspection Reports
Target Agency: Animal Welfare Board of India (AWBI), Chennai
Legal Basis: Right to Information Act, 2005, Section 6(1)
Fee: Rs. 10/- (IPO/DD payable to Secretary, AWBI)
Response Deadline: 30 days under Section 7(1)

SUBJECT: Inspection reports and animal welfare compliance at {facility_name}

QUESTIONS:

1. Please provide certified copies of all inspection reports prepared by AWBI
   or its authorized inspectors for {facility_name} located at {facility_location}
   conducted during the period {start_date} to {end_date}.

2. What is the current registration/recognition status of {facility_name} under
   Section 38 of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960? If registered,
   please provide the registration number and date of last renewal.

3. Has {facility_name} been issued any show-cause notices, warnings, or directions
   under the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 in the last 5 years?
   If yes, provide copies of such notices and the response received.

4. How many complaints have been received by AWBI against {facility_name} from
   {start_date} to {end_date}? Please provide:
   (a) Date of each complaint
   (b) Nature of complaint
   (c) Action taken
   (d) Current status of each complaint

5. What is the approved animal housing capacity for {facility_name} as per
   AWBI guidelines? What was the actual animal count as recorded in the last
   inspection?

6. Does {facility_name} comply with the following AWBI guidelines:
   (a) Minimum space requirements per animal
   (b) Veterinary care provisions
   (c) Feed and water access standards
   (d) Waste management requirements
   (e) Transportation standards (if applicable)
   Please provide specific findings from the most recent inspection.

7. Has AWBI recommended any prosecution under the PCA Act, 1960 or the
   Indian Penal Code (Section 428/429) against {facility_name} or its
   operators? If so, provide details.

8. Please provide a copy of the checklist or inspection protocol used by
   AWBI inspectors for evaluating animal welfare at commercial poultry/dairy/
   livestock operations.

NOTES FOR FILER:
- AWBI head office: 13/1, Third Seaward Road, Valmiki Nagar, Thiruvanmiyur,
  Chennai - 600041, Tamil Nadu
- AWBI was constituted under Section 4 of the PCA Act, 1960
- If facility falls under a specific state, you may also file with the state
  SPCA (Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals)
- First appeal: Secretary, AWBI (within 30 days of non-response or refusal)
- Second appeal: Central Information Commission, New Delhi
