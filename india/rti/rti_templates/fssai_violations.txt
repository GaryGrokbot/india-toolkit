RTI TEMPLATE: FSSAI Food Safety Violations at Meat/Dairy Establishments
Target Agency: Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI)
Legal Basis: Right to Information Act, 2005, Section 6(1)
Fee: Rs. 10/- (IPO/DD payable to Accounts Officer, FSSAI)
Response Deadline: 30 days under Section 7(1)

SUBJECT: Food safety violations at meat and dairy establishments in {district}, {state}

QUESTIONS:

1. How many meat processing plants, slaughterhouses, and dairy processing
   units in {district} district, {state} currently hold valid FSSAI licenses
   under the Food Safety and Standards (Licensing and Registration of Food
   Businesses) Regulations, 2011? Please provide a list with license numbers
   and expiry dates.

2. How many meat and dairy establishments in {district} are operating without
   a valid FSSAI license? What action has been taken against them under
   Section 63 of the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006?

3. How many food safety inspections were conducted at meat and dairy
   establishments in {district} during {year}? Please provide:
   (a) Total number of inspections
   (b) Number of violations detected
   (c) Nature of violations (categorized)
   (d) Penalties imposed

4. Please provide details of all cases where meat or dairy products from
   {district} failed laboratory testing in {year}, including:
   (a) Name of establishment
   (b) Product tested
   (c) Parameters that failed (antibiotic residues, adulteration, microbial
       contamination, aflatoxin M1, pesticide residues, heavy metals)
   (d) Action taken

5. How many cases of milk adulteration (urea, detergent, synthetic milk,
   starch, neutralizers) have been detected in {district} during {year}?
   What action was taken under Section 59 of the FSS Act, 2006?

6. How many meat and dairy establishments in {district} have been found
   violating the Food Safety and Standards (Food Products Standards and
   Food Additives) Regulations, 2011 regarding permissible limits of
   antibiotic residues?

7. Please provide copies of Food Safety Officers' reports for all
   inspections of slaughterhouses in {district} during {year}.

8. How many licenses of meat/dairy establishments in {district} have been
   suspended or cancelled under Section 32 of the FSS Act in the last
   3 years? Provide details.

NOTES FOR FILER:
- FSSAI Head Office: FDA Bhawan, Kotla Road, New Delhi - 110002
- State-level: File with State Food Safety Commissioner's office
- District-level: Designated Officer (DO) under FSS Act
- Key regulations: FSS Act 2006, FSS (Licensing) Regulations 2011,
  FSS (Food Products Standards) Regulations 2011
- First appeal: First Appellate Authority, FSSAI
- Second appeal: Central Information Commission
