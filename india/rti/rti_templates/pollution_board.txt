RTI TEMPLATE: Pollution Data from Animal Agriculture
Target Agency: State Pollution Control Board (SPCB) / Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB)
Legal Basis: Right to Information Act, 2005, Section 6(1)
Fee: Rs. 10/- (IPO/DD payable to Member Secretary, SPCB/CPCB)
Response Deadline: 30 days under Section 7(1)

SUBJECT: Pollution data and compliance status of animal agriculture operations in {district}, {state}

QUESTIONS:

1. How many of the following animal agriculture operations in {district}
   district, {state} hold valid Consent to Establish (CTE) and Consent to
   Operate (CTO) under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act,
   1974 and the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981:
   (a) Poultry farms (broiler and layer)
   (b) Dairy farms (commercial operations with >20 animals)
   (c) Piggeries
   (d) Slaughterhouses and meat processing plants
   (e) Fish/shrimp farms and hatcheries
   Please provide a list with names, locations, and CTO validity dates.

2. Please provide effluent monitoring data for all animal agriculture
   operations in {district} for the period {start_date} to {end_date},
   including:
   (a) BOD (Biochemical Oxygen Demand) levels
   (b) COD (Chemical Oxygen Demand) levels
   (c) TSS (Total Suspended Solids)
   (d) Ammonia/nitrogen levels
   (e) Phosphorus levels
   (f) Coliform count

3. How many complaints regarding pollution (air, water, solid waste, odour)
   from poultry farms, dairy farms, piggeries, and slaughterhouses have
   been received by the Board in {district} from {start_date} to {end_date}?
   Provide complaint-wise details and action taken.

4. How many animal agriculture operations in {district} have been issued
   closure notices or directions under Section 33A of the Water Act or
   Section 31A of the Air Act in the last 3 years? Provide details.

5. Please provide groundwater quality monitoring data for areas within
   1 km of large commercial poultry and dairy operations in {district},
   specifically:
   (a) Nitrate levels
   (b) Ammonia levels
   (c) Coliform count
   (d) TDS (Total Dissolved Solids)
   (e) Heavy metals (zinc, copper, arsenic from feed additives)

6. What is the estimated solid waste (manure, dead birds/animals, processing
   waste) generated by animal agriculture operations in {district}? How
   much of it is treated/processed, and how much is disposed untreated?

7. Have any Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) been conducted for large
   animal agriculture operations in {district} under the EIA Notification,
   2006? If so, provide copies.

8. How many animal agriculture operations in {district} fall in the "Red"
   or "Orange" category as per CPCB classification? Provide the list.

NOTES FOR FILER:
- CPCB: Parivesh Bhawan, East Arjun Nagar, Delhi - 110032
- File with state PCB for state-level data; CPCB for national overview
- Poultry farms with >5000 birds fall under CPCB "Orange" category
- Slaughterhouses are classified as "Red" category under CPCB norms
- Key law: Water Act 1974, Air Act 1981, Environment Protection Act 1986
- NGT (National Green Tribunal) cases may provide additional leverage
