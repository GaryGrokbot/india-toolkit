RTI TEMPLATE: Slaughterhouse Licensing and Compliance Data
Target Agency: District Collector / Municipal Corporation / FSSAI State Office
Legal Basis: Right to Information Act, 2005, Section 6(1)
Fee: Rs. 10/- (IPO/DD payable to relevant authority)
Response Deadline: 30 days under Section 7(1)

SUBJECT: Slaughterhouse licensing, compliance, and inspection data in {district}, {state}

QUESTIONS:

1. Please provide a complete list of all registered/licensed slaughterhouses
   in {district} district, {state}, including:
   (a) Name and address of each slaughterhouse
   (b) License/registration number and issuing authority
   (c) Date of original license and date of last renewal
   (d) License validity period
   (e) Species approved for slaughter
   (f) Approved daily slaughter capacity

2. How many slaughterhouses in {district} comply with the following
   regulations, and how many do not:
   (a) Food Safety and Standards (Licensing and Registration of Food
       Businesses) Regulations, 2011 â€” Schedule 4, Part V
   (b) Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Slaughter House) Rules, 2001
   (c) Municipal bylaws regarding slaughterhouse operations
   (d) State-specific slaughter regulations

3. How many unregistered/unlicensed slaughterhouses have been identified
   in {district} in the last 5 years? For each, provide:
   (a) Location
   (b) Date of identification
   (c) Estimated daily slaughter volume
   (d) Action taken (closure, prosecution, regularization)
   (e) Current status

4. What is the actual daily slaughter count at each licensed slaughterhouse
   in {district} for the last 12 months, species-wise? Does it exceed the
   approved capacity?

5. Please provide copies of all inspection reports for slaughterhouses in
   {district} conducted by:
   (a) Municipal/district veterinary officers
   (b) Food Safety Officers under FSS Act
   (c) Pollution Control Board
   (d) AWBI or SPCA representatives
   for the period {start_date} to {end_date}.

6. Do all slaughterhouses in {district} have:
   (a) Ante-mortem inspection by a qualified veterinarian
   (b) Post-mortem inspection as per FSSAI regulations
   (c) Humane stunning facilities as per PCA (Slaughter House) Rules, 2001
   (d) Effluent treatment plants as per PCB norms
   (e) Cold chain/refrigeration as per FSSAI norms
   Provide compliance status for each.

7. How many animals have been seized during illegal transport to unlicensed
   slaughter in {district} in the last 3 years? Under which provisions
   were cases registered?

8. What is the annual revenue collected from slaughterhouse license fees,
   inspection fees, and related charges in {district} for the last 3 years?

NOTES FOR FILER:
- File with District Collector/Magistrate for district-level data
- File with Municipal Commissioner for municipal slaughterhouses
- Also file with state Food Safety Commissioner for FSSAI data
- PCA (Slaughter House) Rules, 2001: Mandates humane slaughter, ante-mortem
  inspection, adequate space, clean water, and waste treatment
- FSSAI Schedule 4 Part V: Detailed requirements for slaughterhouses
- Transport: Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Transport of Livestock)
  Rules, 1978 and 2001
- Key case: Akhil Bharat Goseva Sangh v. State of A.P. (2006) 4 SCC 162
- Municipal corporations maintain primary licensing authority in most states
