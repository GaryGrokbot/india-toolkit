RTI TEMPLATE: National Livestock Mission / Rashtriya Gokul Mission Spending
Target Agency: Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying (DAHD)
Legal Basis: Right to Information Act, 2005, Section 6(1)
Fee: Rs. 10/- (IPO/DD payable to PAO, Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying)
Response Deadline: 30 days under Section 7(1)

SUBJECT: Expenditure and outcomes under National Livestock Mission and Rashtriya Gokul Mission in {state}

QUESTIONS — NATIONAL LIVESTOCK MISSION (NLM):

1. What is the total central allocation, state share, and actual expenditure
   under the National Livestock Mission (NLM) in {state} for each financial
   year from 2019-20 to 2024-25? Provide component-wise breakup:
   (a) Breed Improvement of Livestock and Poultry
   (b) Feed and Fodder Development
   (c) Innovation and Extension
   (d) Livestock Insurance

2. How many beneficiaries in {state} have received subsidies under NLM for
   establishment of:
   (a) Poultry farms (broiler/layer, capacity-wise breakup)
   (b) Dairy units
   (c) Piggery units
   (d) Goat/sheep rearing units
   Provide district-wise and year-wise data.

3. What is the total subsidy amount disbursed for commercial poultry
   operations in {state} under NLM, including:
   (a) Parent farm subsidy (Rs. 25 lakh cap)
   (b) Rural backyard poultry subsidy
   (c) Feed processing plant subsidy
   Please provide year-wise data from 2019-20 to 2024-25.

4. What outcome/impact assessment has been conducted for NLM in {state}?
   Provide copies of any evaluation reports, utilization certificates,
   or audit findings.

QUESTIONS — RASHTRIYA GOKUL MISSION (RGM):

5. What is the total expenditure under Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM)
   in {state} from 2019-20 to 2024-25? Provide component-wise breakup:
   (a) Gokul Gram establishment and maintenance
   (b) National Kamdhenu Breeding Centre operations
   (c) Artificial Insemination (AI) coverage expansion
   (d) IVF technology deployment
   (e) Progeny testing and pedigree selection
   (f) Sex-sorted semen production

6. How many Gokul Grams have been established in {state}? For each, provide:
   (a) Location and area
   (b) Animal housing capacity
   (c) Current animal population (breed-wise)
   (d) Annual operating expenditure
   (e) Mortality rate

7. What is the total expenditure on artificial insemination under RGM
   in {state}? How many inseminations performed, conception rate achieved,
   and how many calves born (sex-wise breakup)?

8. How many male calves born under the RGM programme in {state} have
   been:
   (a) Retained at Gokul Grams
   (b) Distributed to farmers
   (c) Sold
   (d) Died/lost (with cause of death breakdown)
   This information is critical for assessing the welfare outcomes of
   the programme.

9. What is the per-animal cost of maintaining cattle at Gokul Grams in
   {state}, including feed, veterinary care, and staff costs?

10. Please provide copies of all audit reports by CAG, state AG, or internal
    audit for NLM and RGM schemes in {state} for the period 2019-2025.

NOTES FOR FILER:
- DAHD: Krishi Bhawan, Dr. Rajendra Prasad Road, New Delhi - 110001
- NLM was launched in 2014-15, restructured in 2021-22
- RGM was launched in December 2014 under the DAHD
- Male calf data (Question 8) is strategically critical — reveals the
  hidden cost of dairy intensification programmes
- State implementing agencies vary; may need to also file with state
  animal husbandry department
- Budget data available: Union Budget, Demand for Grants No. 1 (DAHD)
